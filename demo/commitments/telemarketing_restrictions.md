# Telemarketing and Automated Communications Policy

## Document Information
- **Policy ID**: TMC-2024-001
- **Effective Date**: January 1, 2024
- **Scope**: All customer phone numbers and SMS contact information
- **Authority**: Chief Privacy Officer, Regulatory Compliance

---

## 1. Regulatory Framework

This policy ensures compliance with:
- Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA)
- Telemarketing Sales Rule (TSR)
- CAN-SPAM Act (for SMS)
- State-specific telemarketing laws

---

## 2. Purpose Limitation for Contact Information

Customer phone numbers and SMS contact information shall ONLY be used for:

### 2.1 Transactional Communications
- Order confirmations and updates
- Shipping notifications
- Delivery exceptions
- Return/refund status updates
- Security alerts (account takeover, suspicious activity)

### 2.2 Customer-Initiated Support
- Responding to customer service requests
- Following up on open support tickets
- Providing technical assistance
- Resolving complaints

### 2.3 Legal/Regulatory Notifications
- Account terms changes
- Privacy policy updates
- Recall notices
- Regulatory required disclosures

---

## 3. Prohibited Uses

### 3.1 NO MARKETING OR PROMOTIONAL COMMUNICATIONS
Phone numbers and SMS contact info SHALL NOT be used for:
- **Marketing campaigns** of any kind
- **Promotional offers** or discounts
- **Product announcements**
- **Upselling or cross-selling**
- **Surveys or feedback requests** (unless opt-in)
- **Abandoned cart reminders**
- **"We miss you" re-engagement campaigns**

**Rationale**: Customer provided contact info for transaction purposes, not for receiving marketing.

### 3.2 NO AUTOMATED DIALING OR ROBOCALLS
Prohibited:
- Using auto-dialers for outbound calls
- Pre-recorded marketing messages
- Automated SMS blast campaigns
- Predictive dialers

**Rationale**: TCPA violations carry $500-$1,500 per violation penalties.

### 3.3 NO DATA SHARING FOR MARKETING
Phone numbers SHALL NOT be:
- Shared with marketing partners
- Sold to telemarketing firms
- Used for lookalike audience creation
- Included in co-marketing campaigns

---

## 4. Consent Requirements

### 4.1 Express Written Consent Required
For ANY marketing use of phone numbers, customers must provide:
- **Affirmative opt-in** (not pre-checked boxes)
- **Clear disclosure** of marketing purpose
- **Easy opt-out** mechanism
- **Record retention** of consent

### 4.2 Opt-Out Honored Immediately
- Opt-out requests processed within 24 hours
- Phone numbers removed from all marketing lists
- No "grace period" or delayed processing

---

## 5. Data Minimization

### 5.1 Collection Limits
- Do NOT collect phone numbers unless required for transaction
- Do NOT make phone number mandatory for online orders
- Provide alternative contact methods (email)

### 5.2 Retention Limits
- Phone numbers retained only as long as needed for transactions
- Marketing opt-ins expire after 12 months (re-confirmation required)
- Deleted phone numbers purged from all systems

---

## 6. System Requirements

### 6.1 Do-Not-Call Registry Scrubbing
- All phone lists scrubbed against National DNC Registry
- Monthly updates
- Automatic suppression of registered numbers

### 6.2 Access Controls
- Marketing teams SHALL NOT have access to transactional phone lists
- Separate databases for transactional vs. marketing consent numbers
- Audit logs for all phone number access

---

## 7. Examples

### ✅ PERMITTED
- SMS to customer: "Your order #12345 has shipped"
- Call to customer to resolve delivery issue
- Automated security alert: "Suspicious login detected"
- Email asking for phone number WITH clear opt-in checkbox for marketing

### ❌ PROHIBITED
- SMS to customer: "20% off sale this weekend!"
- Call to customer about "exciting new product launch"
- Automated robocall survey about customer satisfaction
- Adding phone numbers from orders to marketing SMS list
- Sharing phone list with partner for co-marketing campaign

---

## 8. Penalties and Enforcement

TCPA violations:
- $500 per unsolicited call/text
- $1,500 per willful violation
- Class action exposure
- FTC enforcement action
